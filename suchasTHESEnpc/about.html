About US

Such as these was founded in March 2019 by Risima Faith Mashila.

Our mission is to eradicate child poverty while bringing a smile to their faces

Just as a single drop cannot fill a bucket, on our own we cannot achieve this.

We collaborate with companies to support Non-profit organisations that work with children.

We partner with individuals who want to make a meaningful contribution to society by donating their time and resources.
